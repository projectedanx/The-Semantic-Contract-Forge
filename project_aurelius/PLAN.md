## Core Intent
Bridging the Causal Intent Gap & Non-Euclidean Latent Space Navigation. Moving beyond statistical correlation in generative models to establish a "causal chain of control" over visual and architectural synthesis.

## 1. Human-AI Synergy: The TACT Lens Analysis
We must define what humans and AI bring to the table in high-dimensional space navigation, utilizing the Technology Affordance and Constraints Theory (TACT).

### The Golden Scar [Φ] (The Value Intersection)
Neither human nor AI can navigate hyper-dimensional latent spaces alone. The synergy emerges when human topological intent directs AI parametric generation.

*   **Human Affordance (The "What" and "Why"):** Humans possess innate *spatial intuition*, *abstract geometric reasoning* (e.g., conceptualizing a hyperbolic manifold), and *teleological intent* (purpose-driven design). We provide the high-level, unstructured "intent" constraint.
*   **Human Constraint:** Humans cannot explicitly calculate the millions of parameter weights required to manifest complex non-Euclidean geometry in a pixel matrix, nor can we manually execute high-fidelity ray tracing in our minds.
*   **AI Affordance (The "How"):** AI excels at *high-dimensional statistical mapping*, *rapid parameter optimization*, and *deterministic execution* of complex rendering pipelines (when guided).
*   **AI Constraint:** AI lacks inherent *causal understanding* of physical laws or geometric absolute truth. It relies on learned, often biased, visual correlations (the "statistical smudge") and struggles with novel spatial topologies not heavily represented in training data.

### Inverting for Emergence
**Strategy:** Instead of prompting for a *result* (e.g., "draw a hyperbolic room"), we prompt the AI to become a *mechanism* that enforces geometric rules. We "invert" the model from a correlative image generator into a deterministic physics and geometry engine constraint solver. The emergence happens when the AI is forced to resolve the human's abstract topological requirements against a strict set of geometric and physical preconditions defined in the contract.

The architecture relies on creating specific Semantic Integrity Constraints (SICs) that act as an API for spatial reasoning.

*   **Implementation Path:** Develop a new contract schema that includes a `geometry_matrix` node, forcing the LLM to output a structured representation of spatial relationships that a downstream rendering pipeline (or an advanced multimodal generation step) must obey.

### Phase 2: Agentic Auto-Optimization (The Plausibility Oracle)

*   **Mechanism:** An agent that receives the initial generated output, analyzes it using external tools (or internal heuristic models acting as proxies for PBR/ray tracing engines), and calculates a "Physical Plausibility Score" (e.g., measuring structural integrity, lighting consistency).
*   **Feedback Loop:** The oracle agent automatically reformulates the prompt to correct detected physical anomalies, feeding it back into the generation cycle until the threshold is met.
*   **Dynamic Provenance Tracking:** The agent monitors the "Semantic Drift" and applies "Attribution Amplification" to explicitly instruct the model to ignore biased training data correlations that contradict the specified geometry.

### Phase 3: Cross-Modal Perceptual Fusion
